# Limitations — perplexity-quota-outage-2026-07-24

- This case documents an operational failure of CFI.co's **own test account**
  (exhausted API credits), not a fault in Perplexity's service or any change
  in how Perplexity treats CFI.co content.
- It is published as a transparency commitment: unsuccessful runs are part of
  the record. It makes **no citation claim** — the recovery run's citation
  results belong to case `citation-run-2026-07-24`.
- Error bodies in `results.json` are token-redacted (`[REDACTED]`) as a
  credential-hygiene precaution; the redaction never alters successful
  responses.
