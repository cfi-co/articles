# <br>Bentley Powers Ahead in Customer Satisfaction: CFI.co Award Winner, Middle East

> Verbatim archived copy. Canonical machine record: `9455-bentley-powers-ahead-in-customer-satisfaction-cfi-co-award-winner-middle-east.json`.

<p style="text-align: justify;"><strong>There has been powerful performance at Bentley Motors, Middle East this past year.</strong> Dubai is the home of a specially designed Bentley workshop which is the largest such facility anywhere in the world. And the welcome extended to the Continental GT, launched in 2012, has resulted in a large slice of Bentley’s worldwide business coming from this part of the world.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The Judging Panel commented that, ‘To say that Bentley customers have nothing to complain about is an extreme understatement. We have no hesitation in naming Bentley as winner of the award ‘Best Customer Satisfaction, Middle East, 2013’. Outstanding customer service ensures that Bentley delivers one of the world’s most desirable driving experiences’.</p>
