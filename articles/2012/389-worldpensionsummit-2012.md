# World Pension Summit 2012

> Verbatim archived copy. Canonical machine record: `389-worldpensionsummit-2012.json`.

<strong>14 -16 NOVEMBER 2012</strong>

WorldPensionSummit is the platform ‘for and by’ Pension Professionals, exchanging knowledge and sharing the latest insights on Securing Sufficient Pension Provision.
<ul>
	<li>Over 300 pensions professionals from 42 countries attended the Summit in 2011</li>
	<li>The WorldPensionSummit is the only objective and strategic event of its kind, with 100% free of ‘sales presentations’</li>
	<li>Interactive sessions, educational platforms, with strategic impact &amp; unmatched networking</li>
	<li>WorldPensionSummit offers a certified program by EBTN - the permanent learning standard for the Financial Services Industry</li>
</ul>
<strong>Key Topics Are: </strong>

Creating a sustainable pension provisionThe importance of corporate pensions and the revival of employee benefits | employer services | Managing risk and maximizing return | Creative next steps related to ageing | Creating essential pension awareness with participants | Downsizing the administrative complexity( (cost | transparency) | Managing the economic outlook (long term vs short term) | The disparities between pension systems and the issue of mobility

Check out the program details on <a href="http://www.worldpensionsummit.com" target="_blank">www.worldpensionsummit.com</a>
